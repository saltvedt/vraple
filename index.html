Vraple

 
 

What is vraple?

vraple is a search engine tool which saves you time by redirecting you directly to the most appropiate search engine for your query.

Why should I use it?

Because most of the time, search engines knows what you want. With vraple, you will save a click nearly everytime you search for something.

What does it actually do?

When you search with vraple, you're immediatly sent to the first search result on google. If you're unhappy with this result and hit the back button, you're sent to a regular google search for that term.

Other than that, 'mathemathical' queries are sent to wolfram alpha, and queries starting with ! are sent to duck duck go.

Do you have access to my search history?

No, all handling of search queries are done in the client, and cannot be accessed from the server. However, vraple do log everytime you visit the site.
